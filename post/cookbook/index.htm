Common and interesting patterns of use

P allows you to establish direct browser-to-browser connections. With P, you can:

  • Establish connections to other browsers using a WebSocket server.
  • Establish connections to other browsers using connections you've established to other browsers.

After a connection is established the middleman is no longer nescessary, there is are no proxies involved.

P relies on young technology -- WebRTC. Currently, only Chrome has the ability to establish the data channels between browsers that P relies on.

Currently onramp, a simple WebSocket server, is used as the signaling channel to establish initial connections.
